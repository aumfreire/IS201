A Little of Adventure into our National Parks

As a student with a passion for both technology and the outdoors, I find my greatest inspiration while exploring the beauty of nature, especially in the incredible landscapes of Utah. Two of my favorite destinations are Zion and Arches National Parks. The towering sandstone cliffs of Zion create a humbling and awe-inspiring experience, while the natural arches and rock formations in Arches feel like stepping into another world. These parks have not only helped me recharge but also sparked my interest in combining data with adventure—documenting travel, analyzing visitor trends, and sharing insights through visual storytelling like the Tableau graph below. Whether hiking Angels Landing or walking through Delicate Arch at sunset, every visit deepens my appreciation for the natural world.


Favorite National Parks and Hikes

  1. Zion National Park
    • Angels Landing – A thrilling chain-assisted climb with incredible canyon views
    • The Narrows – A unique hike through the Virgin River between narrow canyon walls
    • Emerald Pools – A scenic trail to waterfalls and lush hanging gardens
  2. Arches National Park
    • Delicate Arch – The most famous arch in the park, especially beautiful at sunset
    • Devils Garden – A longer trail with multiple arches and fewer crowds
    • Double Arch – Easily accessible and awe-inspiring due to its size and shape
